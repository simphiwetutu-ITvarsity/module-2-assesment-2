DESCRIPTION

What could be more South African than a pizza place started by two students from Maritzburg? They knew that in South Africa you either go big or you go home, so they invented the Triple-Decker® and the Crammed-Crust®. They knew that if they didn’t deliver it hot and fresh, they’d be told off in 11 different languages. That’s how the Hot or Not sticker came about. They knew what a nightmare Joburg traffic is, so they were the first to bring you free delivery and online ordering.
